Privacy Policy

AI Chat Navigator Pro v2.3.0 — Effective March 2026

Information We Collect

AI Chat Navigator Pro only processes data that you explicitly initiate:

  • Selected text from AI chat pages — only when you actively highlight text and assign it to a tag
  • Tag names and colors that you create within the extension
  • Export history metadata (file name, format, date, and highlight count) when you export your highlights

How Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. Data is isolated per hostname, meaning highlights saved on ChatGPT are separate from those saved on Claude or any other platform.

Your data never leaves your browser. There are zero external network requests made by this extension. No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party — ever.

What We Don't Collect

  • No analytics or usage statistics
  • No tracking pixels, cookies, or fingerprinting
  • No telemetry of any kind
  • No external network requests
  • No personal information (name, email, IP address, etc.)
  • No browsing history or activity outside supported AI chat pages

Data Control

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Individual tags and highlights can be deleted directly from the extension side panel
  • All stored data is automatically removed when you uninstall the extension
  • Export history can be viewed and managed within the extension

Third-Party Services

AI Chat Navigator Pro connects to zero third-party services. The extension operates entirely offline after installation. There are no external APIs, no CDNs, no remote resources, and no outbound connections of any kind.

Supported Platforms

The extension operates exclusively on the following AI chat platforms:

ChatGPT Claude Gemini Grok Meta AI Mistral Qwen DeepSeek Perplexity
